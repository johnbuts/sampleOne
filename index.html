Gottfried Leibniz

Date of Birth: July 1st, 1646

Date of Death: November 14th, 1716

Gottfried Leibniz is known for many notable and amazing things, but his two most notable being his contributions to computer science and mathematics. His contribution to computer science was the creation of binary numbers. And his main contributions to math was the creation of infinite calculus. Infinite calculus deals with limits, differentitation, and intergrals.
